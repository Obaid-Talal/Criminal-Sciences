Mission and Goals

Mission Statement

The Criminal Sciences Institute aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to understand and apply the principles of criminal sciences, enhancing their ability to investigate crimes and analyze evidence.

General Objectives

  • Provide comprehensive education in the field of criminal sciences.
  • Develop critical and analytical thinking skills among students.

Specific Goals

  • Train students in modern investigative techniques.
  • Enhance legal and ethical understanding in dealing with crimes.
  • Develop skills in criminal research and analysis.

Importance of Mission and Goals

The mission and goals guide the institute towards achieving its vision and positively impacting society, shaping the future of students and their influence in the community.

Future Vision

We strive to enhance education and training in the field of criminal sciences, updating programs to meet community and job market needs.
